Understanding the Data

What This Shows

This visualization displays how wealth is distributed among German households based on the 2023 Private Household Finance (PHF) study by the German Bundesbank. The data shows the cumulative share of total wealth owned by different percentile groups.

How to Interpret

Use the slider to select any percentile range. For example, selecting 90-100 shows the wealth owned by the richest 10% of Germans. The stark inequality is evident: the top 10% own over half of all wealth, while the bottom 50% own just 3%.

Why This Matters

Wealth distribution affects economic mobility, social cohesion, and policy decisions. Understanding these patterns helps inform discussions about taxation, social programs, and economic inequality in Germany.
